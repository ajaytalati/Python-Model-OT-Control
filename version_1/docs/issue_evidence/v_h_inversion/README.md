# Evidence for issue #4 — V_h structural inversion

Plots referenced from issue #4. Generated by the Phase 1.5 reconnaissance
scripts under `/tmp/phase15_*.py` (re-runnable on demand). All scans
use the SWAT recovery scenario with default parameters from
`_vendored_models/swat/parameters.py`.

| File | What it shows |
|---|---|
| `coarse_vh_vn_scan.png` | Coarse 6×4 V_h × V_n grid, T_0=0.05 (sick), D=60 days. Shows mean T(D), mean E (over horizon), mean E (terminal). Maximum T(D) is at V_h=0, NOT at high V_h. |
| `fine_scan.png` | Fine 11×11 V_h × V_n grid, T_0=0.55 (healthy IC), D=60 days. Resolves the maximum to V_h=0, V_n≈0.4. Shows the inversion clearly. |
| `deterministic_comparison.png` | Same scan run under three noise regimes (stochastic / Stuart-Landau deterministic / fully deterministic). The structural inversion is identical across all three — proves the inversion is in the drift, not the diffusion. |
| `diagnostic.png` | T(t) and E(t) under three constant control vectors (reference / "idealised healthy" v1.2.0 / bounds-max), starting from both T_0=0.05 and T_0=0.55. Shows that more aggressive controls *destroy* E and T. |
